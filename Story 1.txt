In August 2023, Dr. Ayesha Khan traveled from Bengaluru to New York to attend the UN Climate Summit. She represented the Indian Ministry of Environment and delivered a keynote on sustainable energy. Her colleague, Mr. Rahul Verma, joined her on September 2nd. They stayed at the Grand Hyatt Manhattan and visited Columbia University to meet researchers. The trip was sponsored by Tata Trusts, and they returned to India on September 10th.
